Los Angeles Department Management

The Los Angeles Department Management serves as the central administrative body overseeing all law enforcement and emergency service departments in our community. We coordinate interdepartmental operations, establish standardized procedures, allocate resources, and ensure all departments work together effectively to maintain public safety and order throughout Los Angeles.

Our leadership team, headed by General Manager Sittin, works closely with department heads to develop comprehensive strategies, implement best practices, and address community concerns. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement in all our operations.

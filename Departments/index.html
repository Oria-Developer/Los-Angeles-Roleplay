Law Enforcement & Emergency Services

Each department plays a vital role in maintaining public safety and order in our community

Department of Homeland Security

DHS
Federal Agency

The Department of Homeland Security is a federal agency responsible for protecting the United States from various threats, both domestic and international. Our agents work to prevent terrorism, secure borders, enforce immigration laws, and respond to natural disasters and other emergencies.

Federal Bureau of Investigation

FBI
Federal Agency

The FBI is the principal federal law enforcement agency in the United States. Our agents investigate serious federal crimes including terrorism, cybercrime, organized crime, public corruption, civil rights violations, and major white-collar crimes. We work closely with local and state law enforcement agencies.

Bail Enforcement Agency

BEA
State Agency

The Bail Enforcement Agency is responsible for tracking down and apprehending individuals who have skipped bail or failed to appear in court. Our agents work to ensure that defendants comply with court orders and return to face justice. We operate with precision and within legal boundaries.

Park Rangers

PR
Local Agency

Park Rangers are responsible for protecting and managing Los Angeles' extensive park systems. Our duties include law enforcement within park boundaries, conservation efforts, visitor assistance, search and rescue operations, and educational programs about natural resources and wildlife.

Los Angeles Police Department

LAPD
Local Agency

The LAPD is the primary law enforcement agency for the City of Los Angeles. With over 9,000 officers and 3,000 civilian staff, we serve and protect approximately 4 million residents across 473 square miles. Our mission is to safeguard lives and property, reduce crime, and enhance public safety through community partnerships.

California Highway Patrol

CHP
State Agency

The California Highway Patrol is responsible for ensuring the safety, service, and security of California's roadways. Our officers patrol state highways, enforce traffic laws, investigate accidents, and provide assistance to motorists. We work to reduce traffic fatalities and injuries through education and enforcement.

Los Angeles Sheriff's Department

LASO
Local Agency

The Los Angeles Sheriff's Department provides law enforcement services to unincorporated areas of Los Angeles County and contract cities. We operate the county jail system, provide court services, and offer specialized units including SWAT, K-9, and air support. Our mission is to serve with integrity and professionalism.

Los Angeles Fire Department

LAFD
Local Agency

The Los Angeles Fire Department provides fire suppression, emergency medical services, technical rescue, hazardous materials response, and fire prevention services to the City of Los Angeles. With over 3,500 uniformed personnel, we respond to over 500,000 emergencies annually, protecting life, property, and the environment.

Los Angeles Emergency Communications

LACD
Local Agency

The Los Angeles Emergency Communications Department is responsible for handling all emergency and non-emergency calls, dispatching appropriate resources, and coordinating communications between various emergency services. Our dispatchers are trained to handle high-stress situations and provide critical support to first responders.

Los Angeles Security

LAS
Special Agency

Los Angeles Security provides specialized protective services for critical infrastructure, government facilities, and high-profile events. Our highly trained personnel work to prevent security breaches, conduct threat assessments, and ensure the safety of important assets and personnel throughout the Los Angeles area.

Los Angeles Department Management

The Los Angeles Department Management serves as the central administrative body overseeing all law enforcement and emergency service departments in our community. We coordinate interdepartmental operations, establish standardized procedures, allocate resources, and ensure all departments work together effectively to maintain public safety and order throughout Los Angeles.

Our leadership team, headed by General Manager Sittin, works closely with department heads to develop comprehensive strategies, implement best practices, and address community concerns. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement in all our operations.

General Questions

What is Los Angeles Roleplay?

Los Angeles Roleplay (LARP) is a professional and immersive roleplay community based on the Emergency Response: Liberty County (ER:LC) game on Roblox. We aim to provide the most authentic Los Angeles-based roleplay experience with realistic departments, procedures, and community interactions.

Who runs Los Angeles Roleplay?

Los Angeles Roleplay is owned and operated by Sittin, who oversees all community operations, development, and management. The community is built with a focus on quality, professionalism, and immersive roleplay experiences.

Is Los Angeles Roleplay free to join?

Yes! Los Angeles Roleplay is completely free to join and participate in. We believe in creating an accessible community for all roleplay enthusiasts. While we may offer optional VIP perks or cosmetic items in the future, the core gameplay experience will always remain free.

What makes LARP different from other ER:LC servers?

Los Angeles Roleplay stands out through our commitment to:

  • Professional and strict roleplay standards
  • High-quality immersive experiences
  • Active and dedicated staff team
  • Custom systems and features
  • Community-focused development
  • Zero tolerance for rule-breaking and toxicity

Getting Started

How do I join Los Angeles Roleplay?

To join Los Angeles Roleplay, follow these steps:

  1. Join our Discord server using the link on our website
  2. Complete the verification process in the Discord
  3. Read and acknowledge all server rules
  4. Explore the different departments and roles available
  5. Start participating in roleplay scenarios and community events
What departments are available in LARP?

Los Angeles Roleplay features several departments to provide a comprehensive roleplay experience:

  • Los Angeles Police Department (LAPD): Maintain law and order, respond to emergencies
  • Los Angeles Fire Department (LAFD): Handle fires, medical emergencies, and rescues
  • Los Angeles EMS: Provide medical support and emergency care
  • California Department of Public Safety: Oversee operations and ensure roleplay standards

Additional departments may be added as the community grows.

Do I need experience to join?

No prior experience is required to join Los Angeles Roleplay! We welcome roleplayers of all experience levels. Our community is designed to help new members learn and grow. We provide resources, training, and guidance to ensure everyone can enjoy the roleplay experience regardless of their background.

How do I choose a department to join?

When you join Los Angeles Roleplay, you'll have the opportunity to explore different departments and find the one that best fits your interests. Consider:

  • Your preferred roleplay style (law enforcement, medical, firefighting, etc.)
  • The type of scenarios you enjoy most
  • Your availability and commitment level
  • Your long-term roleplay goals

You can always change departments later if you find another role more appealing.

Rules & Guidelines

What is the Zero Tolerance Policy?

Our Zero Tolerance Policy means that we strictly enforce all server rules without exceptions. This includes:

  • Automatic 24-hour ban for first-time RDM/VDM offenses
  • Permanent ban for any form of hate speech or discrimination
  • Immediate action against trolling, harassment, or toxicity
  • Strict enforcement of roleplay quality standards

This policy ensures that our community maintains a professional, respectful, and immersive environment for all members.

What are the consequences for breaking rules?

Consequences for rule violations depend on the severity and frequency of the offense:

  • Minor offenses: Warning or temporary suspension
  • Moderate offenses: Temporary ban (24 hours to 1 week)
  • Serious offenses: Extended ban (1 week to 1 month)
  • Severe offenses: Permanent ban with no appeal

All decisions are made at the discretion of our staff team based on the specific circumstances of each case.

Can I appeal a ban or suspension?

Yes, you can appeal bans or suspensions through our official appeal process. However, please note:

  • Appeals must be submitted through the proper channels (not via DMs)
  • You must wait at least 24 hours after receiving a ban before appealing
  • Permanent bans for severe offenses (hate speech, discrimination) are typically not appealable
  • You must provide valid evidence and reasoning for your appeal

Appeal decisions are final and made by our senior staff members.

What is considered Fail Roleplay (FRP)?

Fail Roleplay (FRP) refers to actions that break immersion or don't make logical sense in a roleplay context. Examples include:

  • Ignoring realistic consequences for actions
  • Breaking character in serious situations
  • Performing actions your character wouldn't reasonably do
  • Using out-of-character knowledge in character
  • Ignoring realistic limitations (health, resources, etc.)

We expect all members to maintain realistic and immersive roleplay at all times.

Technical Questions

What are the system requirements to play?

Since Los Angeles Roleplay is based on Roblox, the system requirements are minimal:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 or later, macOS 10.10 or later
  • Processor: Any processor made after 2005
  • Memory: 1GB RAM minimum
  • Graphics: DirectX 9 minimum and Shader Model 2.0
  • Storage: At least 20MB of free space
  • Internet: Stable broadband connection

Most modern computers and devices can run Roblox without issues.

I'm experiencing lag or connection issues. What can I do?

If you're experiencing performance issues, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet connection and restart your router if needed
  2. Close other applications and browser tabs to free up system resources
  3. Lower your graphics settings in Roblox (Settings > Graphics Mode)
  4. Clear your Roblox cache (Settings > Reset Character)
  5. Try connecting from a different network if possible
  6. Update your graphics drivers

If issues persist, contact our support team with details about your problem.

Are custom CAD systems available?

Yes! We're developing custom CAD (Computer-Aided Dispatch) systems programmed by Sittin specifically for Los Angeles Roleplay. These systems will enhance the roleplay experience with:

  • Custom dispatch interfaces
  • Department-specific features
  • Realistic emergency response workflows
  • Integrated communication systems

The CAD systems are currently in development and will be released to the community soon.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

To report bugs or technical issues:

  1. Join our Discord server and navigate to the support channel
  2. Open a support ticket with detailed information about the issue
  3. Include screenshots or video evidence if possible
  4. Describe what you were doing when the issue occurred
  5. List any error messages you received

Our development team will investigate and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

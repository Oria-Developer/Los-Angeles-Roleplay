Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How do I join the Discord server?

Click the "Join Discord" button on our website and complete the verification process to access all channels.

What is the response time for support tickets?

We aim to respond to all support tickets within 24-48 hours. Discord support is typically faster.

How do I appeal a ban?

Submit a support ticket with the "Ban Appeal" category and include all relevant details about your situation.

Where can I find the server rules?

Our complete rules are available on the Rules page of our website and in the Discord server.

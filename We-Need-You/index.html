About the "We Need You" Program

The "We Need You" program is our 2025 initiative to recruit talented designers who can help create the visual identity of our growing Los Angeles Roleplay community.

As we rebuild and expand, we need dedicated team members who can produce high-quality vehicle liveries and character uniforms that will make our community stand out in the Roblox universe.

5+ Designers Needed
7 Days to Apply
2 Liveries Per Week
12K Robux Monthly

Program Requirements

To maintain quality and consistency in our community, we have specific requirements for all program participants:

Design Quality

All liveries and uniforms must meet our "good quality" standards. Subpar work may result in termination from the program.

Weekly Output

You must create at least two liveries every 7 days. Consistency is key to maintaining your position in the program.

Active Participation

Regular communication and timely delivery of assignments are required. Inactivity may result in removal from the program.

All Departments Needing Designs

We need designs for ALL departments in our roleplay community. Each requires distinctive liveries and uniforms.

Police Department

Patrol cars, SUVs, SWAT vehicles, and all officer uniforms

Fire & Rescue

Fire engines, ambulances, rescue vehicles, and firefighter uniforms

Medical Services

Ambulances, medical response units, and EMT/paramedic uniforms

Federal Agencies

FBI, DEA, Homeland Security vehicles and agent uniforms

K-9 Unit

K-9 vehicles and handler uniforms

How to Apply

Ready to become part of our design team? Follow these steps to apply for the "We Need You" program:

Step 1: Review Requirements

Make sure you understand the program requirements and can commit to the weekly design output.

Step 2: Prepare Your Portfolio

Gather examples of your previous design work (Roblox or other platforms). If you don't have previous work, create sample designs to showcase your skills.

Step 3: Complete the Application Form

Fill out our official Google Form with your information and portfolio links.

Step 4: Join Our Discord

Connect with our community and stay updated on your application status.

Step 5: Trial Assignment

If selected, you'll complete a small trial assignment to demonstrate your design capabilities.

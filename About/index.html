Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving Roblox community

Building Los Angeles Roleplay

Los Angeles Roleplay was founded in 2025 with a simple vision: to create the most immersive and authentic Los Angeles-based roleplay experience on the Roblox platform.

What started as a small group of dedicated roleplayers has grown into a vibrant community of over 100 active members, featuring 8+ specialized departments and round-the-clock roleplay opportunities.

Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and community engagement has made us one of the most respected roleplay communities in the Roblox universe.
